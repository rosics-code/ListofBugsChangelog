I am Rosics! Nice to meet you, I have been Scratching since 2021, I made this website to unclutter my sticky to get rid of a long list of Changelogs, and to not deal with Scratch bugs while editing the Changelogs, I hope you like the website! ^^

Don't like what this website looks like or how it functions? Go to my GitHub repository page and open an Issue or a PR, I will accept it if it's good or deny it if it isn't good, don't worry if I deny it, I don't hate you, and you can always try again with a new Issue or PR! ^^
